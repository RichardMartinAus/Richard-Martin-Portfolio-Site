About me

Hi, I'm Richard. I'm originally from South Africa but moved to Melbourne in late 2021. My wife was offered a position here and it was an offer we couldn't refuse!

I have spent the last 19 years working for in-house and boutique design agencies all over South Africa with a focus on layout and finished art. I've worked on everything from business cards to billboards and everything between. With a change in location I thought what better time to switch gears and change the direction of my career.

So here I am, at 41 years of age, learning to code! I'm busy building my portfolio as I add skills to my stack, so keep an eye on my work section as it expands with the knowledge I gain!
